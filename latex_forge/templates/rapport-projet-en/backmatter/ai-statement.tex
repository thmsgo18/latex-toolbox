\begin{center}
  {\normalsize\bfseries Note on the Use of Artificial Intelligence}
\end{center}

\vspace{0.4em}

\noindent In the preparation and writing of this report, we made use of
artificial intelligence tools, notably \textbf{Claude (Anthropic)} and
\textbf{ChatGPT (OpenAI)}. Their use was limited to the following aspects:

\begin{itemize}[topsep=4pt, itemsep=2pt]
  \item \textbf{Writing assistance:} rephrasing, improving clarity, and correcting
        grammar and syntax in English.
  \item \textbf{Brainstorming:} generating ideas and exploring different approaches
        during the design and development phases.
  \item \textbf{Technical support:} assistance in identifying and resolving bugs,
        as well as guidance in the implementation of certain functionalities.
\end{itemize}

\noindent All analyses, design choices, implementations, and conclusions
presented in this report are the result of the authors' own work.
The use of artificial intelligence tools did not replace the authors'
understanding, reasoning, or decision-making process.

\noindent All intellectual contributions and authorship of this project
remain exclusively attributed to its creators.
